About

 I originally started my journey into the programming world when I started to
go to collage for front end web development. However in the later half or the
semester as I was taking a introduction to programming class, I discovered that
I really enjoyed programming, and so, over the course of the summer break, I spent
a lot of my time learning and using JavaScript and decided to become a full stack
developer.

  As I was getting the credits needed for my Certificate in Web Application
Development I discovered with only a couple of extra classes I could also get a
Certificate in Software Development.

  After getting the credits needed for both of Certificates complete I figured
that I would need some sort of experience to be able to get hired as a developer. I
had heard at this point how hard it was to get a entry level job, so instead of doing
what everybody else was doing, I decided the best way for me to get experience was
for me to build my own project.

  Knowing how big of a undertaking that this would end up being, I committed
myself to writing at least one line of code a day, so that I wouldn't just give up
and stop on the project half way through. Fortunately my plan worked and I was able
to complete my Dungeons and Dragons AI Game Master, which you can find at
home.wizardchat.xyz.

  Now I am working on a DC20 (DC20 is a table top role playing game similar to
Dungeons and Dragons) character creator while looking for a job.
